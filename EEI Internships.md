# **Empowering Earth Institute Volunteer Internship Program**

## **Program Overview:**

Welcome to the Empowering Earth Institute's (EEI) Volunteer Internship Program - a transformative journey designed not just as an internship, but as a launchpad for future world-changers. This isn't your typical coffee-fetching, behind-the-scenes internship. This is a front-row seat to global impact, a hands-on experience with cutting-edge technologies, and a deep dive into the world's most pressing challenges. If you're looking to make a dent in the universe, you're in the right place.

  

### **Duration:**

3 to 6 months (with the possibility of extension)

  

### **Location:**

Remote with potential opportunities for on-site work at EEI centers or partner locations in the future

  

### **Key Benefits:**

1. Gain hands-on experience in a rapidly growing sector.

2. Network with professionals and experts in the field.

3. Receive mentorship and guidance from EEI team members.

4. Participate in EEI workshops and training sessions.

5. Obtain a certificate of completion and recommendation letter upon successful completion.

  

---

  

## **Program Highlights:**

  

1. **AI-Powered Impact:** Harness the power of artificial intelligence to address global challenges, from climate change to social inequities.

2. **Global Network:** Collaborate with a diverse team of visionaries, tech gurus, and change-makers from around the world.

3. **Real-World Projects:** Work on impactful projects that have tangible outcomes, not just theoretical exercises.

4. **Holistic Growth:** Beyond technical skills, immerse yourself in personal development sessions, mindfulness practices, and leadership workshops.

5. **Epicenter of Innovation:** Be at the forefront of technological and social innovation, merging AI with sustainable practices for a better world.

  

## **Potential Roles & Responsibilities:**

  

**1. Research & Development Intern:**

- Assist in researching AI tools and technologies.

**3. Sustainability & Permaculture Intern:**

- Assist in the design and implementation of permaculture projects.

- Collaborate with local communities to promote sustainable living.

  

**4. Education & Training Intern:**

- Assist in the development of educational materials and curricula.

- Support the organization of workshops and training sessions.

- Engage with students and provide feedback.

  

**5. Grant Writing & Fundraising Intern:**

- Research potential grant opportunities.

- Assist in the preparation of grant proposals.

## **Eligibility Criteria:**

1. Passionate about EEI's mission and values.

2. Strong communication and interpersonal skills.

3. Ability to work independently and collaboratively in a team.

4. Relevant academic background or experience in the chosen internship role.

5. Proficiency in English; knowledge of additional languages is a plus.

  

## **Expectations:**

1. Commit a minimum of 15-30 hours per week.

2. Attend regular check-in meetings with the assigned mentor/supervisor.

3. Complete tasks and projects within specified deadlines.

4. Maintain professionalism and adhere to EEI's code of conduct.

5. Participate daily in Discord and on Project Management tool

  

## **Feedback & Evaluation:**

Interns will receive regular feedback on their performance. At the end of the internship, a final evaluation will be conducted, and successful interns will receive a certificate of completion and a recommendation letter.

  

## **Note:**

While this is a volunteer internship, EEI is committed to ensuring a rewarding experience for all interns. Potential opportunities for future employment or collaboration may arise based on performance and organizational needs.

## **Post-Application Process:**

  

1. **Interview:** Shortlisted candidates will be invited for a virtual interview.

2. **Onboarding:** Successful applicants will undergo a dynamic onboarding process, introducing them to EEI's mission, tools, and teams.

3. **Mentorship:** Each intern will be paired with a mentor from EEI's core team, ensuring guidance and support throughout the journey.

4. **Feedback & Growth:** Regular check-ins and feedback sessions will ensure continuous learning and growth.

5. **Completion:** At the end of the internship, standout interns will receive a certificate of excellence, letters of recommendation, and potential opportunities for further collaboration with EEI.
